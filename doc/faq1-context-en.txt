FAQ about eyeshadow — full list of questions and answers (received 21.05)
Do they dust when picked up?
The textures are high quality and pressed, they do not dust. Shimmers apply softly and transmit color beautifully.
Do they need a base to look like the reference?
No base is needed. The shadows give maximum saturation. High pigmentation and shimmers open perfectly, staying all day.
Do they crease in the eyelid fold after 4–6 hours?
No. The formula is long-wear, does not crease, and retains intensity and glow.
How many grams does one shadow weigh? — 2.5 grams per refill.
Are there refillable pans?
Yes, the pans are refillable. The shade number is on the back of each refill.
Is the texture like Viseart?
Better quality: does not dust, high pigmentation, soft application, even coverage.
Which brush should you use?
Crease: CTR 0149, 0657, 0196.
Lid: 0530 (goat), 0145 (sable), 0656 (sable), 0174 (raccoon), 0709 (squirrel), 0585 (squirrel).
Which hair is best for blending?
Squirrel — airy blending: 0708, 0188, 0507.
Goat — active blending, rich color: 0597, 0598, 0181.
Country of origin? — South Korea.
Shelf life? — 3 years.
Are shimmers in every palette? — No, only Vintage and Enigma.
Tested on animals? — No, cruelty-free.
Does the pan color match the shade on skin? — Yes, high pigmentation, shades translate well.
Do they tint the eyelid strongly?
They give rich pigment but do not tint strongly. Most are easy to remove with makeup remover.
Do they fallout? — No. Mattes lay cleanly, shimmers stay on the lid.
Do dark shades patch? — No, with proper application.
Do mattes leave spots? — No, they apply evenly and blend easily.
Are shimmers oily or dry?
The texture is dry, but application feels soft. They give an even luminous finish.
Do the glitters fall under the eyes? — No. Better over a primer or applied with a finger.
Can they be used for day and evening makeup? — Yes, the palettes are universal.
Is the gold shade too yellow? — A soft warm gold with a noble metallic finish.
Does blue turn muddy with brown? — No, it keeps its purity. Layer gradually and use a clean brush for transitions.
Are they suitable for sculpting?
Barocco shades are good for light sculpting. Vintage shimmers are good for cheeks and inner corners.
Will the lid snap off? — Durable plastic, reliable mechanism, daily use.
How fragile are they? — Not fragile, well pressed. Don’t drop them or press on them.
Will they survive shipping? — Yes, they are tightly fixed and arrive intact.
Refill weight? — 2.5 grams.
Are they for beginners or only professionals? — Both. Easy to blend for beginners, enough pigment for pros.
Which color type is the nude Barocco good for?
Universal: light skin uses taupes, warm tones use beige-browns and golds, high-contrast complexions use deeper shades.
Is the pigment visible from the first touch? — Yes, high pigmentation on first pass. It becomes brighter with layering.
